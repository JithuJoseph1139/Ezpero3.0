General Guidelines

FOR ALL EVENTS

  • ELIGIBILITY: Participation is open to all Higher Secondary, UG, and PG students of recognized institutions.
  • TEAM REGISTRATION: Teams must have the required number of participants as per individual event rules.
  • ID PROOF: Participants must carry a valid institutional ID card for verification.
  • PUNCTUALITY: Teams must report to the venue 15 minutes prior to the scheduled start time. Latecomers may face disqualification.
  • CODE OF CONDUCT: Participants are expected to maintain discipline and sportsmanship throughout the event. Misconduct will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • USE OF MOBILE PHONES: Mobile phone usage during events is strictly prohibited, unless specifically required for the activity.
  • CHEATING OR UNFAIR MEANS: Any form of cheating, plagiarism, or rule violation will result in instant disqualification.
  • DECISION OF JUDGES: The judges' and event coordinators' decisions will be final and binding in all matters.
  • WALKOVER RULE: If a participant/team does not show up or fails to respond within the given time, the opponent/team will be declared the winner by walkover.
  • RESULT DECLARATION: Results will be declared immediately after the event, and participants must respect the final verdict.
  • PROPERTY CARE: Any damage to venue property or event equipment will be the responsibility of the concerned individual/team.
  • SUBMISSION OF PROOF (IF APPLICABLE): In events requiring proof of results (like eFootball or BGMI), screenshots or video proofs must be submitted promptly to the admins.
